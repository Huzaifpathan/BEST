About Our BEST

BEST Education (Bhaskaracharya Education In Science And Technology) is a secular organization dedicated to change in Indian Students' Mind by focusing on basic science and technology education in the belief that education is a critical requisite for change mind in science and technology. In keeping with this focus, our volunteers are involved with and support projects that are secular and have an education-related component to them.

The objectives of BEST are:

  • To provide practical education of science to Indian students.
  • To Encouraging science activities in schools and colleges.
  • To raise the required human and other resources to achieve the group objectives.
  • Adopt highest standards of governance to emerge as a leading knowledge and technology driven, innovative and scalable development institution.
  • To provide quality education in rural areas.

We are focusing on practical knowledge of science. Developing creative minds of students.
